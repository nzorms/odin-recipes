Fried Porkchop

Juicy and delicious porkchops

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon seasoned salt and ground pepper
  • Pork
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • Cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 cup canola oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • smashed new potatoes

Step-By-Step

  1. salt and pepper both sides of the porkchop
  2. combine flour and some cayenne, salt, black pepper
  3. heat oil over medium-high heat
  4. Cook porkchops, 2 to 3 minutes on first side, flip and cook until golden brown
